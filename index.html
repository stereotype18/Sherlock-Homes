Getting Started

How do I log into the phone and CRM systems each shift?
Log into the Faith system using your standard ID and password, then launch the softphone application and set your status to "Available" at the start of your shift. Contact IT immediately if you can't access any required system.
Where do I find my schedule, and how do I request a shift swap?
Your schedule is published directly in Faith. To swap a shift, email your Team Lead (TL) at least 48 hours before the affected shift. The TL approves the request and forwards it to Workforce Management (WFM) for processing.
What should I do on my first day?
Review all onboarding materials and training resources provided to you, and make sure you have full system access and knowledge readiness before taking any live customer calls.

Handling Calls

What's the standard greeting and verification process?
Greet every customer professionally, and always verify their details comprehensively before discussing any account information — this step is mandatory regardless of how familiar the caller sounds.
How do I handle an upset or angry customer?
Stay calm and professional, let the customer fully explain their concern without interrupting, acknowledge their frustration with empathy, and keep the conversation focused on solutions and clear next steps.
What if I don't know the answer to a customer's question?
Search this knowledge base first. If the answer still isn't there, consult your Team Lead. Only place the customer on hold if it's truly necessary, and never guess on policy, billing, or compliance questions.
Can I put a customer on hold, and for how long?
Yes — but always ask permission first. Keep hold times under 2 minutes whenever possible; if you need longer, offer the customer a callback instead of leaving them waiting.
What's the target average handle time (AHT)?
AHT targets are set by management, but quality and first-call resolution always take priority over rushing a call just to hit the number.

Escalations & Tools

When should I escalate a call to a supervisor?
Escalate when the customer's request exceeds your approval authority, legal concerns are raised, safety concerns come up, or the customer specifically asks for a manager.
How do I submit a ticket for a technical issue?
Log into the IT Helpdesk portal, select the appropriate support category, include your workstation details, and attach a screenshot of the error if one is available.
What do I do if the CRM or phone system goes down?
Notify the designated incident communication channel immediately, switch to the approved manual backup procedure, and only resume normal operations once IT has given official confirmation.
Where do I find product or policy information during a call?
This knowledge base is your primary source for policy, product, and process information — check it here first before improvising an answer.

Schedule, Breaks & HR

How do I request time off?
Submit your leave request to your Team Lead, ideally at least two weeks in advance. Your TL approves it and forwards it to WFM; final approval depends on staffing and operational needs.

Who do I contact for payroll issues?
Contact the Payroll Team directly, include your Employee ID, and provide clear, specific details of the issue so it can be investigated.

Performance & Quality

How is my call quality scored?
Calls are reviewed regularly against five core areas: greeting, accuracy, empathy, compliance, and resolution. You'll receive feedback through coaching sessions and standardized scorecards.
Where can I see my performance dashboard, and what metrics matter?
Review your performance metrics with your Team Lead. Key metrics tracked include Average Handle Time (AHT), Customer Satisfaction (CSAT), Quality Score, Schedule Adherence, and Productivity.

What if a holiday isn't on this list, like Thaipusam?
If a Malaysian public holiday isn't listed as Mandatory or Optional above, it is not officially recognized as a Monitor Sdn Bhd holiday unless management makes an official announcement. Examples of holidays that currently fall into this not-recognized category include Thaipusam, Awal Muharram, Nuzul Al-Quran, Maulidur Rasul, Gawai Dayak, and Kaamatan.
