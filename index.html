Getting Started

How do I log into the phone and CRM systems each shift?
Log into the CRM using your company SSO credentials, then launch the softphone app and set your status to "Available" at the start of your shift. Contact IT if you're locked out.
Where do I find my schedule or request a shift swap?
Your schedule is posted in the workforce management portal every Friday for the following week. Shift swaps must be submitted at least 48 hours in advance and approved by your team lead.
What should I do on my first day?
Check in with your team lead, complete your system access checklist, and review the onboarding playbook shared in your welcome email before taking live calls.

Handling Calls

What's the standard greeting script?
"Thank you for calling [Company], this is [Name], how can I help you today?" Always confirm the customer's name and account before discussing account details.
How do I handle an upset or angry customer?
Stay calm, let them finish speaking, acknowledge their frustration, and focus on next steps. If the call becomes abusive or you can't resolve it, escalate to your supervisor.
What if I don't know the answer to a customer's question?
Let the customer know you're checking, search the knowledge base, or place them on a brief hold to consult a team lead. Never guess on policy, billing, or compliance questions.
Can I put a customer on hold, and for how long?
Yes — ask permission first. Keep hold times under 2 minutes; if it will take longer, offer a callback instead of leaving them waiting.
What's the target average handle time (AHT)?
Target AHT is set by your team lead and reviewed monthly. Quality and resolution always take priority over rushing a call to hit the number.

Escalations & Tools

When should I escalate a call to a supervisor?
Escalate for refund/compensation requests above your approval limit, legal threats, safety concerns, or when a customer explicitly asks for a manager.
How do I submit a ticket for a technical issue?
Open the internal IT helpdesk, select "Agent Tools" as the category, and include your workstation ID and a screenshot of the error.
What do I do if the CRM or phone system goes down?
Report it immediately in the team incident channel, switch to the documented manual backup process, and wait for an all-clear from IT before resuming normal call handling.
Where do I find product or policy information during a call?
Use the internal knowledge base — pinned articles cover the most common topics. If something is missing or outdated, flag it to your team lead.

Schedule, Breaks & HR

How do I request time off?
Submit a request through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance. Approval depends on staffing coverage for your requested dates.
What's the break and lunch policy?
Two paid 15-minute breaks and one unpaid 30-minute lunch per 8-hour shift, scheduled by your team lead to maintain call coverage.
Who do I contact for payroll issues?
Email payroll@yourcompany.com with your employee ID and a description of the issue. Most discrepancies are resolved within one pay cycle.

Performance & Quality

How is my call quality scored?
A sample of calls is reviewed monthly against a quality rubric covering greeting, accuracy, empathy, and resolution. You'll receive scorecards with feedback from your team lead.
Where can I see my performance dashboard?
Your KPI dashboard (AHT, CSAT, quality score, adherence) is available in the workforce management portal, updated daily.
